Egg Fried Rice

Recipe Decription

Egg fried rice is a quick, savory Asian stir-fry dish made by tossing cooked rice—ideally day-old—with scrambled eggs, oil, soy sauce, and aromatics like green onions and garlic in a high-heat wok.

Ingredients

  • Leftover overnight white rice
  • soy-sauce
  • vegetables of choice
  • cooking oil
  • spices of choice - paprika, etc.
  • 3-4 eggs

Steps

  1. Chop the veggies into nice small pieces
  2. Add oil to wok and heat on a high heat. Progress to add chopped veggies
  3. Once sauteed, remove from the wok. Add fresh oil and add eggs to scramble
  4. Once eggs are scrambled, remove and bring back sauteed veggies
  5. Add a bit more oil then add the rice
  6. Slowly start to mix the rice and veggies with a ladle. Add your spices
  7. Add the eggs, and a dash of soy sauce
  8. Stir-fry everything and plate to enjoy once done.
