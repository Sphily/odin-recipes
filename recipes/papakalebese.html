Papa ka Lebese

Recipe Decription

"Pap and milk" is a popular, quick, and comforting breakfast staple, particularly in Nigeria and other parts of West Africa.

It consists of fermented cereal pudding (pap) combined with milk, often paired with other foods for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Papa maize meal
  • Fresh Milk
  • water

Steps

  1. Pour the water into a pot to boil over a medium to low heat
  2. When it starts to boil, slowly start adding the papa maize meal while stirring it in until it starts to thicken.
  3. Leave to "tjoatl" and keep coming back to the pot every 10-15mins to stir
  4. Once you're satisfied with, turn stove off and leave to cool
  5. Add 1-2 serving spoons to bowl and add in preferred amount of milk
  6. Enjoy!
