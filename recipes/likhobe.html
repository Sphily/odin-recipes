Likhobe

Recipe Decription

Likhobe is a traditional, nutritious, and hearty stew originating from Lesotho, a mountainous nation surrounded by South Africa.

It is a staple dish in Basotho cuisine, often described as a mixture of beans, grains, and sometimes meat or vegetables, traditionally eaten as a comforting, warming meal.
